**HOW TO RUN**:

1. Open Visual Studio.
2. Click "Open a project or solution".
3. Navigate to the project folder and open the .sln file.
4. Open Program.cs and hit run.
   5.The game window will open and the animation of the rockets will start automatically.


**Program Description**:

Two rockets animate upward across a dark background. Each rocket is built
from multiple parts (body, nose cone, fins, and fire) that all move together,
demonstrating hierarchical modeling.

Rocket 1:  Red body | White cone | Gray fins | Orange fire  (speed: 1.5)
Rocket 2:  Blue body | Black cone | Dark gray fins | Red fire  (speed: 2.0)

When a rocket flies off the top of the screen, it reappears at the bottom
and continues looping.


**Interactive Controls**:

ESC key          - Exit the application

There are no other interactive controls. The animation runs on its own.

**Software Needed to Run**:

- .NET SDK
- MonoGame 3.8+
